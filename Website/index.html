What if...

--------------------------------------

What if you take the chance, what if you risk your heart,
what if you crash it into someone who genuinely inspires you,
and they choose you? What if they love you the way you always desired to be loved?
What if they make you breakfast in bed on Sundays,
and hug your broken pieces back together,
and bring you soup when you're sick, and fill your life with the sunniest,
most tender kind of happiness? What if they grow you,
and encourage you, and teach you that love was always meant to be soft?
That it was always meant to feel the way it feels when you are with them?
What if they make you understand why it never worked out with anyone who came before them?

What if they stay?
